Riccardo Cadei

Machine Learning Researcher

Hello World,
I am Riccardo, a Visiting Graduate Student at Harvard University conducting research on Causal Inference and Machine Learning while I am completing my M.Sc. in Data Science at EPFL. I am passionate in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence but even more enchanted by human brains. My ambition is to use these technologies to leave a positive contribution in health care and in the health of our planet. I have a Mathematical background and I am interested as much in new theories as in their real-world applications. In particular, I am recently re-approaching to Machine Learning through the lens of Causality and I am truly fascinated by the ambition and potential of this paradigm shift from standard Statistical Learning.

This is my personal website where you can practically read about my research interests and background through my past experiences and accomplished projects. Enjoy your reading and do not hesitate to contact me for any discussion.
