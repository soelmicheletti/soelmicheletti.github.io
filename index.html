Hey!
I'm a Master Student in Data Science at ETH Zurich, currently visiting the John Quackenbush group at Harvard University. My thesis focuses on the inference of Gene Regulatory Networks: this has connections to matrix factorization and causal inference, as well as important applications in biology. On a broader level, I have a passion for problem solving and I'm interested in everything connected to Machine Learning and algorithms.

During the first year of my Bachelor in Computer Science at ETH, after winning the Odd Fellow Prize at the National Competition by Schweizer Jugend Forscht, I had the chance to present my high school project at the Taiwan International Science Fair. Check out the interviews about the project and the experience in Taipei.

I'm also a proud member of the Swiss Study Foundation, and I'm always looking forward to new challenges, experiences, and interesting opportunities.
